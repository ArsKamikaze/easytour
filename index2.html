Amazing Stockholm combines history and modernity. Enjoy the medieval charm of the Old Town and modern architecture. Lapland with its northern lights and the Archipelago with its islands. Tour offers a unique journey into the heart of Lapland, where clear skies and remoteness from city lights create ideal conditions for stargazing. You will visit Abisko National Park, have the chance to witness the Northern Lights, and spend a night in a camp with transparent tents for comfortable star viewing. Additionally, you will enjoy scenic hikes, campfire dinners, and an introduction to local culture. This trip will provide unforgettable experiences and a chance to feel the magic of Swedish nature.
